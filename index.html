Straight Smiles - no waiting rooms!

The Wire Wagon is California’s fully licensed, fully mobile orthodontic practice— bringing straight teeth and good vibes straight to you! Whether we're parked under the shade of a tree at your local park or pulling up curbside to your home, our mission is to make orthodontic care as easy and enjoyable as possible. Licensed by the California Dental Board, we provide professional, high-quality orthodontic services in a relaxed, outdoor setting that skips the stress of traditional office visits. Think of us as your neighborhood smile-mobile—making orthodontics convenient, comfortable, and just a little more fun.

Meet Dr. Lane

Dr. Lane is a proud graduate of Loma Linda School of Dentistry, receiving his DDS as well as his Master's degree in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics.

For over 15 years Dr. Lane has never lost his passion for creating beautiful smiles, offering each patient affordable, personalized, and precise Orthodontic treatment.

When not in the office, you can usually find Dr. Lane at the beach with his family. He is known as a perfectionist with a witty sense of humor. There is rarely a dull moment when Dr. Lane is around!
